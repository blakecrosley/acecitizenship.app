# Ace Citizenship Blog Writer

Specialized blog writing for Ace Citizenship, a U.S. citizenship test prep iOS app. Content focuses on helping immigrants pass the naturalization test and understand American civics.

## Company Configuration

- **COMPANY-FACTS.yml** - Ace Citizenship app details, platforms, target audience
- **TONE-VOICE.md** - Encouraging, empowering, patriotic but accessible voice
- **INTEGRATION-POINTS.md** - When and how to mention the app
- **SEO-STRATEGY.yml** - Target keywords for citizenship test prep

## Content Philosophy

Ace Citizenship's blog should feel supportive and practical. Every post should leave readers feeling more confident and better prepared, never overwhelmed or anxious.

### Writing Approach

1. **Start with encouragement** - Acknowledge the journey, then provide value
2. **Be practical** - Focus on actionable study tips and real preparation strategies
3. **Respect the reader** - These are adults pursuing a significant life goal
4. **End with confidence** - Reinforce that success is achievable with the right preparation

### Content Types We Write

- **Test Prep Guides** - Study strategies, question breakdowns, practice tips
- **Civics Explainers** - U.S. history and government made accessible
- **Process Guides** - Naturalization timeline, interview prep, what to expect
- **Success Motivation** - Encouragement for the citizenship journey

## Specific Rules for Ace Citizenship

1. **Never fear-monger** - Don't emphasize failure rates or scary statistics
2. **Cite official sources** - USCIS.gov is the primary authority
3. **Stay practical** - Every post should include something actionable
4. **Be inclusive** - Remember readers come from diverse backgrounds and English proficiency levels
5. **Current information** - The test changed in 2020, use current 128 questions (100 civics + 28 reading/writing)

## Key Facts to Know

- The civics test has 100 questions, applicants are asked up to 10 and must answer 6 correctly
- Reading and writing portions use vocabulary from 28 sentences
- Test is offered in English (with some age/residency exceptions)
- Applicants 65+ with 20+ years residency may take simplified test
- Current president, state officials, and representatives vary by location
