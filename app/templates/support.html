Frequently Asked Questions

How many questions are in the app?

Ace Citizenship includes all 128 official USCIS civics questions updated for 2025. These are the same questions used in the actual naturalization interview.

How does spaced repetition work?

When you answer a question incorrectly, it goes back into the deck and will appear again later. This helps you focus on questions you need to practice more, making your study time more efficient.

Why do I need to select my state?

Some civics questions ask about your state's senators, governor, and capital. By selecting your state, the app automatically fills in the correct answers for these location-specific questions.

What about my U.S. Representative?

Unlike senators and governors which are state-wide, your representative depends on your specific congressional district. You'll need to enter your representative's name manually. You can find this information at house.gov.

Is the app really free?

Yes! Ace Citizenship is completely free to download and use. There are no in-app purchases or subscriptions required to access all features.

How do I reset my progress?

You can restart your study session at any time from within the app. This will shuffle all questions and let you start fresh.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your data (settings, progress, state selection) is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or transmit any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
